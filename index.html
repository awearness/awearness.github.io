What is awearness?

awearness is a anwesome (at least we think it is) wristband created at the JugendHackt Hackathon (an awesome Hackathon for humans between 11 and 18 years in Berlin).

Actually it is quite simple: It is a 3d-printed wristband which connects to a smartphone via Bluetooth Low Energy. Then the smartphone is able to trigger a vibration or a blinking LED of the wristband. The Idea is to remind you of certain things and there are several ideas:

  • Vibration when a surveillance camera is nearby
  • Vibration when you're in front of a supermarket and need to buy something
  • Vibration when you're driving to fast with your car
  • ...
