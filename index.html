What is awearness?

awearness is an awesome (at least we think it is) wristband created during JugendHackt 2014 (a yearly hackathon for humans between 11 and 18 years in Berlin).

Actually it is quite simple: A 3d-printed wristband connects to a smartphone via Bluetooth. The smartphone then is able to trigger the built-in vibration motor or LEDs. The idea is to remind you or make you aware of certain things. For example:

  • a surveillance camera is nearby
  • you are in front of a supermarket and need to buy something
  • you're driving to fast
  • ...
